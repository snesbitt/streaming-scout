01

what it is

Ranks what to watch next from Susan's real, multi-year Netflix and Prime Video history — every title since 2023, including BritBox and PBS Masterpiece — matched against her stated taste (spy, action, British procedurals; no sci-fi, fantasy, rom-coms). Also tracks premieres months ahead, and what she's watching or seeing right now.

Refreshes itself Monday mornings via a scheduled task, or sooner if Susan asks Claude to resync directly.

02

the stack

Static HTML, plus one small Netlify Function for the dismiss button — simpler infrastructure than the other two sites, not the same. Deploys via GitHub: pushing to main triggers Netlify's Git-connected continuous deployment automatically, the same governance model as the other two sites, whether Susan pushes directly or the Monday scheduled task does.

This site, today

  • Static HTML, no build step
  • One Netlify Function: dismiss, Blobs-backed, bounded
  • GitHub repo, auto-deploy on push
  • Dismiss syncs instantly; permanent exclusion still needs a chat confirm

Vinyl Scout & The Fitness Log

  • Full Netlify Functions backend
  • Automated backups & health checks
  • GitHub repo, auto-deploy on push
  • Writes happen without a chat round-trip

Dismissals now sync across every device the moment you click them. Making an exclusion permanent through the next weekly rebuild still takes one extra step. Two gaps remain versus the sibling sites: automated backups, and a health check that confirms each week's update actually published.
